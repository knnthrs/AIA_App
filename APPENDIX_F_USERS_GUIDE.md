APPENDIX F: USER'S GUIDE

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for using the AIA Fitness Gym Management System. The system consists of a mobile application for gym members and a web-based dashboard for administrative staff.

_______________________________________________________________________________

PART 1: MOBILE APPLICATION (For Gym Members)

### 1.1 Getting Started

#### 1.1.1 Creating an Account

**Step 1: Download and Install**
- Download the AIA Fitness Gym app from the provided source
- Install the application on your Android device (requires Android 8.0 or higher)
- Open the app to begin

**Step 2: Sign Up**
1. On the welcome screen, tap **"Sign Up"**
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number (Philippine mobile format: 09XX-XXX-XXXX)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character)
   - Confirm Password
3. Check the box to agree to Terms and Conditions
4. Tap **"Sign Up"** button
5. Wait for confirmation message

**Step 3: Complete Your Profile**
1. After successful registration, you'll be directed to profile setup
2. Select your gender
3. Enter your personal information:
   - Age
   - Weight (kg)
   - Height (cm)
4. Select your fitness level:
   - Sedentary (little or no exercise)
   - Lightly Active (light exercise 1-3 days/week)
   - Moderately Active (moderate exercise 3-5 days/week)
   - Very Active (hard exercise 6-7 days/week)
   - Extremely Active (very hard exercise & physical job)
5. Choose your fitness goal:
   - Lose Weight
   - Build Muscle
   - General Fitness
   - Improve Endurance
6. (Optional) Add any health issues or physical limitations
7. Tap **"Save"** to complete your profile

---

### 1.2 Logging In

**For Regular Login:**
1. Open the app
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Enter your password
4. Select **"User"** as user type
5. Tap **"Login"**

**Forgot Password?**
1. On the login screen, tap **"Forgot Password?"**
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Tap **"Send Reset Link"**
4. Check your email for password reset instructions
5. Follow the link to create a new password

---

### 1.3 Main Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard with:

**Top Section:**
- Profile picture and name
- Current membership status
- Membership expiration date (if active)

**Quick Actions:**
- **Membership** - View or purchase membership plans

**Activity Feed:**
- Recent workouts completed

### 1.4 Purchasing a Membership

**Step 1: View Available Plans**
1. From the dashboard, tap **"Membership"**
2. Browse available membership packages:
   - **Daily Pass** - Single day access
   - **Monthly Plans** - 1, 3, or 6 months
   - **Personal Training Packages** - Includes PT sessions with a coach

**Step 2: Select a Plan**
1. Tap on the plan card to view details
2. Review:
   - Duration
   - Price
   - Features included
   - Number of PT sessions (if applicable)
3. Tap **"Select"** if you want this plan

**Step 3: Review and Agree**
1. Review your selected plan details
2. Read the Terms and Conditions
3. Check the box to agree
4. Tap **"Confirm"**

**Step 4: Choose Coach (For PT Packages Only)**
1. A dialog will appear asking how to select a coach:
   - **"Let the system choose"** - Automatically assigns an available coach
   - **"Choose my coach"** - Browse and select from available coaches
2. If choosing manually:
   - Browse coach profiles
   - View their specialties and ratings
   - Tap on your preferred coach
   - Tap **"Select Coach"**

**Step 5: Complete Payment**
1. You'll be redirected to the PayMongo payment page
2. Choose your payment method:
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - GCash
   - GrabPay
   - Other supported payment methods
3. Enter payment details
4. Review payment summary
5. Tap **"Pay Now"**
6. Wait for payment confirmation

**Step 6: Confirmation**
- Once payment is successful, your membership activates immediately
- You'll receive a confirmation message
- Your dashboard will update with new membership status
- Your QR code will be generated for gym check-ins

---

### 1.5 Accessing Your QR Code

**What is the QR Code?**
Your QR code is your digital gym pass. Staff will scan it when you enter the gym.

**To Access Your QR Code:**
1. From the dashboard, tap **"QR Code"**
2. Your unique QR code will be displayed
3. Show this to staff at the gym entrance

**Tips:**
- Save a screenshot for offline access
- QR code works even without internet connection
- Each code is unique to your account
- Don't share your QR code with others

---

### 1.6 Using the Workout Planner

#### 1.6.1 Generating Your Personalized Workout

**How It Works:**
The app creates a custom workout plan based on your:
- Fitness level
- Goals
- Health conditions
- Current week of progression

**To Generate Your Workout:**
1. From the dashboard, tap **"Workout"**
2. The system will automatically check:
   - If you have an active workout for the current week
   - If your profile has changed since the last workout
   - If you've completed your previous workout
3. If a new workout is needed, the app will generate one automatically
4. You'll see a list of exercises with:
   - Exercise name and image/GIF
   - Sets and reps
   - Instructions
   - Estimated duration

**To Adjust Workout Difficulty:**
1. On the workout list screen, tap the settings icon (if available)
2. Adjust the difficulty slider:
   - Move left to make it easier
   - Move right to make it harder
3. Tap **"Regenerate"** to create a new workout with adjusted difficulty
4. The app will remember your preference for future workouts

---

#### 1.6.2 Starting a Workout Session

**Step 1: Review Your Workout**
1. On the workout list screen, review all exercises
2. Check the total duration estimate
3. Make sure you have necessary equipment

**Step 2: Start the Session**
1. Tap **"Start Workout"** button
2. The app will show a ready countdown (3, 2, 1...)
3. Voice guidance will announce the first exercise

**Step 3: Follow Along**
1. Each exercise screen shows:
   - Exercise name and animation
   - Current set and rep count (or timer)
   - Instructions
   - Progress bar
2. The app will:
   - Count down exercise duration
   - Announce remaining time (voice)
   - Show rest periods between exercises
   - Automatically move to next exercise

**During the Workout:**
- **Pause** - Tap the pause button if you need a break
- **Skip** - Swipe or tap skip to move to next exercise
- **Previous** - Go back to previous exercise if needed
- **Info** - Tap info icon to view detailed instructions

**Step 4: Complete the Workout**
1. After the final exercise, you'll see a completion screen
2. Summary includes:
   - Total duration
   - Exercises completed
   - Calories burned (estimate)
3. Tap **"Finish"** to save the workout
4. Your workout history is automatically updated

### 1.10 Troubleshooting

**Problem: Can't log in**
- Check your email and password are correct
- Ensure you have internet connection
- Try "Forgot Password" to reset
- Contact gym staff if issue persists

**Problem: QR code not displaying**
- Check your membership is active
- Ensure you've completed payment
- Try logging out and logging back in
- Contact support if issue continues

**Problem: Workout won't generate**
- Ensure your profile is complete
- Check internet connection
- Try force-closing and reopening the app
- Update to the latest version

### 3.5 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Can I use the app without a membership?**
A: You can create an account and view packages, but you need an active membership to access workouts and gym facilities.

**Q: How long does it take for my membership to activate?**
A: Memberships activate immediately after successful payment. Your QR code is generated within seconds.

**Q: Can I change my workout difficulty?**
A: Yes, you can adjust difficulty in the workout settings. The app will regenerate your plan based on the new difficulty level.

**Q: What happens when my membership expires?**
A: You'll receive notifications 7, 3, and 1 day before expiration. After expiration, your QR code won't work for check-ins, but you can still log in and purchase a new membership.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I can't use my membership?**
A: All memberships are non-refundable as stated in Terms and Conditions. However, you may discuss special circumstances with gym management.

**Q: How do I change my assigned coach?**
A: Contact the gym admin through the app or in person. Admins can reassign coaches through the web dashboard.

**Q: Is my payment information safe?**
A: Yes, all payments are processed through PayMongo, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment gateway. We never store your card details.

**Q: Can I share my QR code with friends?**
A: No, each QR code is unique and tied to your account. Sharing QR codes violates the Terms of Service and may result in membership suspension.

**Q: What if I lose my phone?**
A: Log in from a new device using your email and password. Your membership and QR code will be available on the new device.

**Q: How accurate are the workout calories burned estimates?**
A: Calorie estimates are approximations based on exercise intensity, duration, and your profile data. Individual results may vary.
