## üìã Complete Exercise Details

### 1. Marching in Place
**Instructions**:
- Stand tall with feet hip-width apart
- Lift your right knee to hip height
- Lower it and immediately lift your left knee
- Swing your arms naturally as you march
- Keep a steady, comfortable pace
- March for prescribed repetitions per leg

**Safety Notes**:
- Start slowly if you're a beginner
- Keep your core engaged
- Land softly, don't stomp
- Breathe naturally

---

### 2. Arm Circles
**Instructions**:
- Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
- Extend arms out to the sides at shoulder height
- Make small circular motions forward for half the reps
- Reverse direction and circle backward
- Gradually increase the size of the circles
- Keep your core engaged and posture upright

**Safety Notes**:
- Start with small circles
- Keep shoulders relaxed
- Don't rush the movement
- Stop if you feel pain

---

### 3. Leg Swings (Forward & Back)
**Instructions**:
- Stand next to a wall for balance
- Swing your outside leg forward and back in controlled motion
- Keep your torso upright and core engaged
- Start with small swings and gradually increase range
- Complete reps on one leg, then switch sides
- Move smoothly without jerking motions

**Safety Notes**:
- Use wall for balance
- Don't force the range of motion
- Keep standing leg slightly bent
- Build up swing height gradually

---

### 4. Standing Torso Rotations
**Instructions**:
- Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
- Place hands on hips or clasp in front of chest
- Rotate your torso to the right, keeping hips forward
- Return to center, then rotate to the left
- Keep movements smooth and controlled
- Feel the gentle stretch in your core and back

**Safety Notes**:
- Keep hips facing forward
- Don't twist forcefully
- Move slowly and controlled
- Stop if you feel back pain

---

### 5. Bodyweight Squats (Leg Activation)
**Instructions**:
- Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out
- Keep your chest up and core engaged
- Lower down by bending knees and pushing hips back
- Go as low as comfortable while keeping heels down
- Push through heels to return to standing
- Keep knees tracking over toes throughout

**Safety Notes**:
- Don't go too deep at first
- Keep weight in heels
- Don't let knees cave inward
- Stop above parallel if needed

---

### 6. Wall Push-Ups (Chest Activation)
**Instructions**:
- Stand arm's length from a wall
- Place hands on wall at shoulder height and width
- Keep body straight from head to heels
- Bend elbows to bring chest toward wall
- Push back to starting position
- Control the movement both ways

**Safety Notes**:
- Step further from wall if too easy
- Keep core tight
- Don't let hips sag
- Breathe out as you push

---

### 7. Scapular Squeezes (Back Activation)
**Instructions**:
- Stand or sit with good posture
- Arms at sides or bent at 90 degrees
- Squeeze shoulder blades together toward spine
- Hold for 2 seconds
- Release slowly
- Keep shoulders down, away from ears

**Safety Notes**:
- Don't hunch shoulders up
- Focus on the squeeze
- Breathe naturally
- Gentle movement, not forced

---

### 8. Hip Circles (Secondary Activation)
**Instructions**:
- Stand with hands on hips, feet shoulder-width apart
- Make circular motions with your hips
- Move in a clockwise direction for half the reps
- Reverse and go counterclockwise
- Keep upper body relatively still
- Make smooth, controlled circles

**Safety Notes**:
- Start with small circles
- Keep knees soft
- Balance issues? Hold onto something
- No pain in hips or back


2. `createActivationExercise()` - Selects workout-specific activation
3. `createSecondaryActivation()` - Adds hip circles for full prep
4. `analyzeMainWorkoutBodyParts()` - Determines which activation to use

### Exercise Data Structure
Each exercise is created as an `ExerciseInfo` object with:
- Unique ID (e.g., "warmup_marching")
- Name
- Body parts targeted
- Equipment (always "body weight")
- Target muscles
- Placeholder GIF URL
- Detailed instructions (step-by-step)

Then wrapped in `WorkoutExercise` with:
- Sets: Always 1
- Reps: 8-20 based on fitness level
- Rest: 20-30 seconds
- Order: 1-6
